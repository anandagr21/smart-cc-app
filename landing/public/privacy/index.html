Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Card Optimizer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and name from Google. We do not receive or store your Google password.

Credit Card Information. You manually add cards to your wallet by selecting from our catalog. You provide: card name, bank, annual fee amount, billing cycle dates, and optional spend tracking amounts. We never ask for or store your credit card numbers, CVV, expiry dates, PINs, or bank login credentials.

Transaction Information. When you log a transaction, you provide: merchant name, transaction amount, payment mode (online/offline/UPI/international), and optionally which card you used. Transactions are entered manually by you — we do not access your bank statements, SMS, or email.

Usage Data. We collect basic usage information to improve the Service: which features you use, interaction patterns (e.g., recommendation views, card additions), and app crash reports via Sentry. This data is anonymized and aggregated where possible.

Device Information. We may collect your device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service — including showing you which card is best for each transaction
  • Calculate reward comparisons, fee waiver progress, and portfolio optimization insights based on the cards you've added
  • Send you relevant notifications (e.g., fee waiver deadline approaching)
  • Detect, prevent, and fix technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with the app to guide product improvements

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to production data is strictly limited to essential personnel.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Service:

  • Google OAuth — for sign-in authentication only. Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process.
  • Sentry — for crash reporting and error monitoring. Sentry receives anonymized error data and device information.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. No Bank Linking

Card Optimizer does not link to your bank accounts, read your SMS messages, access your email, or use account aggregator frameworks. All card and transaction data is entered manually by you. This means:

  • We never see your bank balance or full transaction history
  • We cannot initiate transactions or payments
  • You control exactly what data lives in the app

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data collection (e.g., usage analytics)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.
